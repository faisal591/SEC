Help

This system works to facilitate the tasks related to the IT department of the "Saudi Electricity Company"

How does the system work?

- This system works to record tasks and save them in a database, as files can also be uploaded. The purpose is to raise the efficiency of work and dispense registration through paper or any work that would raise the cost.

How can I Record a "Delivery Note" and save it in the Database?

To record the delivery note, follow these instructions:

First: Log in to the system.

Second: From the menu choose "Delivery Note".

Third : You will be asked to enter the following data:

Employee Name

Employee ID

Department ...etc

Fourth: Click "Save" and will save directly to the database

How can I Add a new Employee and save the information in the Database?

To Add a new Employee , follow these instructions:

*First: Log in to the system.

*Second: From the menu choose "Add a new Employee".

*Third : You will be asked to enter the following data:

-First Name

-Last Name

-National ID ...etc

*Fourth: Click "Save" and will save directly to the database

How can I Upload files and save it in the Database?

To Upload files , follow these instructions:

*First: Login to the system.

*Second: From the menu choose "Upload".

*Third : You will be asked to enter your password

*Fourth: Click "choose" and will open your files window

*Fifth: Select the files you want to upload to the database , and click "Enter" and a message will appear telling you that the files have been successfully uploaded

Why the upload function is protected?

The file upload feature is protected because files uploaded are important and may include personal data such as National ID, bank accounts and others

How can I use the Database?

To use the Database , follow these instructions:

*First: Login to the system.

*Second: From the menu choose "Database".

*Third :You will see the contents of the database, where you can edit, delete and add data

+ You can also view and modify the uploaded files by clicking on "Storage"
